Rule 9-4 2023-24 NFHS Basketball Rules Page 62 SECTION 4 TRAVEL, KICK, FIST, BALL ENTERS BASKET FROM BELOW A player shall not travel with the ball, as in Rule 4-44, intentionally kick it, as in Rule 4-29, strike it with the fist or cause it to enter and pass through the basket from below.

NOTE:

Kicking the ball is a violation only when it is an intentional act; accidentally striking the ball with the foot or leg is not a violation.

PENALTIES:

The ball is dead when the violation occurs.

1.

When the opponent is awarded possession in their frontcourt, the throw-in will be at one of the four designated spots nearest the violation.

(Diagram 5) 2.

When the opponent is awarded possession in their backcourt, the throw- in will be from the designated out-of-bounds spot nearest the violation.

SECTION 6 JUMP BALL A player shall not violate any provision of the jump ball (6-3).

If both teams simultaneously commit violations during the jump ball or if the official makes a bad toss, the toss shall be repeated.
