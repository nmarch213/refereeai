Rubber, cloth or elastic bands may be used to control hair.

Hard items, including, but not limited to, beads, barrettes, bobby pins and other adorn- ments in the hair that are securely fastened to the head and do not pres- ent an increased risk to the player, teammates or opponents are allowed.
