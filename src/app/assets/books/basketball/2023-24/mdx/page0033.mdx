A ball is at the disposal of a player when it is:

SECTION 5 BASKET, CHOICE ART.

1 .

.

.

A team's own basket is the one into which its players try to throw or tap the ball.

ART.

2 .

.

.

Each team's basket for practice before the game and for the first half shall be the one farther from its team bench.

ART.

3 .

.

.

The teams shall change baskets for the second half.

ART.

4 .

.

.

If by mistake the officials permit a team to go the wrong direction, when discovered all points scored, fouls committed, and time consumed shall count as if each team had gone the proper direction.

Play shall resume with each team going the proper direction based on bench location.
